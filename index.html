My Story

...The Journey So Far

I've had a lifelong obsession with electronic media and have possessed an uncanny ability to fundamentally understand new technology as I interacted with it. As a child, I was constantly tinkering with my home computer: setting up networking to connect to remote servers, configuring new hardware and helping others with the repair and maintenance of their systems.

Throughout my life I have used this ability to quickly retain information, pairing it with strong communication skills to become a concierge at prestigious hotels throughout NYC, to teach English in South America, and to write for several local TV news outlets.

Recently, I've decided to pursue a career in software devlopment so that I can continue to learn more about what technology can do and what I can do with it. I want to create software that enhances our daily lives and enriches our future.
